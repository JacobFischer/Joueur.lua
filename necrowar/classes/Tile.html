Class Tile

A Tile in the game that makes up the 2D map grid.

Fields

self.corpses The amount of corpses on this tile.
self.isCastle Whether or not the tile is a castle tile.
self.isGoldMine Whether or not the tile is considered to be a gold mine or not.
self.isGrass Whether or not the tile is considered grass or not (Workers can walk on grass).
self.isIslandGoldMine Whether or not the tile is considered to be the island gold mine or not.
self.isPath Whether or not the tile is considered a path or not (Units can walk on paths).
self.isRiver Whether or not the tile is considered a river or not.
self.isTower Whether or not the tile is considered a tower or not.
self.isUnitSpawn Whether or not the tile is the unit spawn.

self.numGhouls The amount of Ghouls on this tile.
self.numHounds The amount of Hounds on this tile.
self.numZombies The amount of Zombies on this tile.
self.owner Which player owns this tile, only applies to grass tiles for workers, NULL otherwise.
self.tileEast The Tile to the 'East' of this one (x+1, y).
self.tileNorth The Tile to the 'North' of this one (x, y-1).
self.tileSouth The Tile to the 'South' of this one (x, y+1).
self.tileWest The Tile to the 'West' of this one (x-1, y).
self.tower The Tower on this Tile if present, otherwise nil.
self.unit The Unit on this Tile if present, otherwise nil.
