Class Game

Mine resources to obtain more value than your opponent.

Fields

self.name The name of this game, "{game_name}".
self.bombPrice The monetary price of a bomb when bought or sold.
self.bombSize The amount of cargo space taken up by a Bomb.
self.bombs Every Bomb in the game.
self.buildingMaterialPrice The monetary price of building materials when bought.
self.currentPlayer The player whose turn it is currently.
self.currentTurn The current turn number, starting at 0 for the first player's turn.
self.dirtPrice The monetary price of dirt when bought or sold.
self.fallDamage The amount of damage taken per Tile fallen.
self.fallWeightDamage The amount of extra damage taken for falling while carrying a large amount of cargo.
self.gameObjects A mapping of every game object's ID to the actual game object.
self.ladderCost The amount of building material required to build a ladder.
self.ladderHealth The amount of mining power needed to remove a ladder from a Tile.
self.largeCargoSize The amount deemed as a large amount of cargo.
self.largeMaterialSize The amount deemed as a large amount of material.
self.mapHeight The number of Tiles in the map along the y (vertical) axis.
self.mapWidth The number of Tiles in the map along the x (horizontal) axis.
self.maxShielding The maximum amount of shielding possible on a Tile.
self.maxTurns The maximum number of turns before the game will automatically end.
self.maxUpgradeLevel The highest upgrade level allowed on a Miner.
self.miners Every Miner in the game.
self.orePrice The amount of money awarded when ore is dumped in the base and sold.
self.oreValue The amount of value awarded when ore is dumped in the base and sold.
self.players List of all the players in the game.
self.session A unique identifier for the game instance that is being played.
self.shieldCost The amount of building material required to shield a Tile.
self.shieldHealth The amount of mining power needed to remove one unit of shielding off a Tile.
self.spawnPrice The monetary price of spawning a Miner.
self.suffocationDamage The amount of damage taken when suffocating inside a filled Tile.
self.suffocationWeightDamage The amount of extra damage taken for suffocating under a large amount of material.
self.supportCost The amount of building material required to build a support.
self.supportHealth The amount of mining power needed to remove a support from a Tile.
self.tiles All the tiles in the map, stored in Row-major order.
self.timeAddedPerTurn The amount of time (in nano-seconds) added after each player performs a turn.
self.upgradePrice The cost to upgrade a Miner.
self.upgrades Every Upgrade for a Miner in the game.
self.victoryAmount The amount of victory points (value) required to win.
